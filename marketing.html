Country Manager – SEO & Affiliate Marketing

Online Dating Comparison – International (UK, USA, Australia, France & the Netherlands)

Online dating is a highly competitive niche and most online dating sites fail to deliver what they promise. LeadingDatingSites.co.uk, DatingSitesReviews.com.au and DatingSitesReviewed.com are top online dating site review portals in the UK, Australia and United States respectively. Through carefully researched and optimized expert reviews, they receive high levels of organic traffic which generate revenue via affiliate deals with top players in the online dating industry.

***A diverse and challenging role that covered many aspects of digital marketing. Our expertise was solidified by the annual research, writing and publication of industry studies.
