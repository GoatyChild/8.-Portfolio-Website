Samantha Fox – History

Oregon native Samantha Fox founded Brightline Media in 2010. A student of Beloit College, Samantha graduated with a B.A. in Communications and Marketing in 2008. As luck would have it, that was when the United States experience the worst economic downturn since the Great Depression. Many new graduates faced the prospect of unemployment. Fresh-faced and unfazed, Samantha refused to accept this fate. She picked herself up and devised a new plan.

Even with the economy in tatters, Samantha knew the digital world was changing things. New technologies and processes were emerging – developing at lightning speed. Seemingly overnight, many businesses were well behind the times.

For Samantha, this was empowering. These new technologies promised fresh opportunities. She began her long campaign of approaching companies, pitching her services and offering to streamline their marketing efforts. Her goal: to establish long-term, sustainable awareness of their brand, whilst reducing unnecessary costs.

Over eight years later, companies and clients of every size have availed of Samantha’s expertise. Tiny local shops to huge corporations like Florsheim, Menards, Sears, General Electric, Target and many more, Samantha has assisted and led national and international marketing campaigns in a diverse range of industries. Her knowledge of communication technologies knows no bounds.

It is through servicing all types of business over her career that Samantha and her team have brought Brightline Media to the fore in effective marketing for the new age. With a vast range of skills and expertise, clients can have confidence in receiving the highest quality of work and direction.
